# Salesforce

## Weaknesses
- Total cost of ownership is notoriously high: licences + Salesforce Admin + customisation dev can exceed $150k/year for a 50-person team.
- Implementation complexity: average enterprise deployment takes 6–18 months and requires a certified SI partner.
- UI/UX is dated and dense — SDR adoption rates are consistently below 60% without dedicated training programmes.
- No native conversation intelligence — requires purchasing Gong or Chorus as an add-on ($$$).
- Slow innovation cycle: AI features (Einstein) lag behind purpose-built tools by 12–18 months.
- Over-engineered for SMBs and mid-market — most customers use < 20% of paid features.

## Our Advantages vs Salesforce
- **10× faster to deploy**: Auralis median go-live is 11 days; Salesforce enterprise deployments average 9 months.
- **Conversation AI included**: real-time objection coaching, sentiment analysis, and persona detection are built in — no third-party add-on required.
- **Cost**: a 20-seat Auralis team costs ~$8k/year vs ~$60k+ for equivalent Salesforce Sales Cloud + Gong.
- **Higher SDR adoption**: Auralis UI is designed for reps first — 87% weekly active usage in beta cohort vs industry 58% average.
- **No SI dependency**: self-serve onboarding with a dedicated CSM for the first 90 days.

## Integration Story
- Salesforce → Auralis: full Salesforce data export via standard API (Contacts, Accounts, Opportunities, Activities).
- Auralis provides a guided Salesforce migration wizard — import completes in under 4 hours for up to 100k records.
- For teams that must retain Salesforce (compliance, enterprise mandate): Auralis offers a bi-directional sync that writes call summaries, objection labels, and sentiment scores back into Salesforce Activity records.
- No SFDC admin required for the Auralis-side configuration.
