# HubSpot

## Weaknesses
- Steep pricing tiers: jumps from Starter ($50/mo) to Professional ($890/mo) with no middle ground.
- CRM and Marketing Hubs are sold separately — full-stack price easily exceeds $3,000/mo for mid-market.
- Limited AI capabilities: no real-time objection coaching, no conversation intelligence.
- Customisation ceiling: deep workflow automation requires Professional or Enterprise tier.
- Onboarding complexity: power users report 6–12 week ramp time to unlock advanced features.
- Reporting is siloed — cross-hub analytics require manual data joins or third-party BI tools.

## Our Advantages vs HubSpot
- **Conversation Intelligence built-in**: Auralis classifies objections and adapts pitch strategy in real time — HubSpot has no equivalent.
- **Unified pricing**: single subscription covers CRM, email, pipeline, and AI coaching with no hub lock-in.
- **Faster onboarding**: median time-to-value is 11 days vs HubSpot's 6–12 weeks (internal benchmark, 200 customers).
- **Native RAG knowledge base**: reps get cited proof points mid-call without leaving the platform.
- **Adaptive sentiment detection**: Auralis detects frustrated prospects and triggers human handoff automatically.
